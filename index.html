Welcome to Foodilicious

Cutting food waste is a delicious way of saving money, helping to feed the world and protect the planet

Food wastage is one of the biggest problems which we face in our society. We throw away large amount of foods when millions of people are deprived of it.

We are looking at a way which tries to avoid food wastage in a manner which is profitable to both the producer (such as restaurants) and consumer at the same time with SAP acting as an intermediary between the two.
